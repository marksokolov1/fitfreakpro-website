Turn your coaching process into one organized client experience.

FitFreak Pro is built for independent trainers, hybrid in-person and online trainers, online fitness coaches, and small coaching businesses that need a cleaner way to manage clients.

When FitFreak Pro fits

You manage roughly 5 to 30 clients and your workflow is split across spreadsheets, text messages, shared notes, folders, and video links.

What the app helps with

Build your own exercise library, use your own videos, invite clients, assign workouts, set nutrition targets, and review submitted progress information.

What it is not

FitFreak Pro is not a generic consumer workout app, a marketplace of workout plans, a replacement for the trainer, or a full enterprise gym-management platform.

Why clients benefit

Clients get one consistent place to view assigned workouts, exercise details, nutrition targets, and the progress expectations set by their coach.
