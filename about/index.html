About

Built to make independent coaching easier.

FitFreak Pro is being built as a practical tool for coaches who are tired of managing programming, videos, nutrition targets, and client updates across disconnected tools.

Founder-led product support

The strongest FitFreak Pro customer is an independent trainer who wants a cleaner way to deliver individualized programs without forcing clients through scattered files, messages, and notes.

The product is intentionally focused on coach-owned content, simple client management, and a clearer app experience for clients.

What FitFreak Pro is

A coach-led utility platform for exercise libraries, workout assignment, nutrition targets, client invitations, and progress review.

What FitFreak Pro is not

It is not a medical service, healthcare provider, generic consumer workout app, marketplace of plans, or replacement for a personal trainer.
